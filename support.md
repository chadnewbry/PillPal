## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a medication?
Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter your medication name, dosage, and schedule, then save. PillPal will remind you at the times you set.

### How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual medications within the App, or delete all data by removing the App from your device. You can also contact us at [chad.newbry@gmail.com](mailto:chad.newbry@gmail.com) to request full data deletion.

### Is my data private?
Yes. Your medication data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy) for full details.

### Can I change my reminder times?
Yes. Tap on any medication in your list, then edit the schedule to adjust your reminder times.

### Is PillPal a medical device?
No. PillPal is a personal organization tool to help you remember your medications. It does not provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
